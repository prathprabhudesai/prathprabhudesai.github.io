About Me

I am a Graduate Student at North Carolina State University and completed my first year this spring. My main research interests are Computer Vision, Pattern Recognition and Machine Learning. See my work on Projects page.

In this summer, I am working at VastAccess Inc, a Raleigh born start-up, as a Development & Operations Intern where we build web-based Electronic Medical Record Systems.

Before coming to NC State, I was working as a Senior Server Engineer at Vodafone Shared Services. As a part of the Wintel Infrastructure Management team, I worked on various administrative issues of Windows Servers, V-spheres and Citrix Servers.

I did my bachelors from Pune Institute of Computer Technology in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering. For my final year undergraduate project, I got the opportunity to work under M. SelvaBalan at Central Water and Power Research Station as a Research Project Intern where I worked on developing the Image Processing based Test and Measurement software systems for civil, hydraulics and fluid related applications. To know more check out my Research page.

Music

Recently I have taken to the genre of Progressive Rock Music on the recommendation of a friend and have really started to appreciate it. Do checkout some videos and recordings on my Music page.
