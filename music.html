I have been playing indian classical Harmonium since 13 years. I am a disciple of Mr. Rajesh Puranik and Pt. Tulsidas Borkar. I have accompanied many classical singers and tabla players.

I was an active part of PICT Art Circle. I represented PICT as a musician and as a music arranger and designer in prestegious Purushottam Karandak and Firodiya Karandak. I was the Inter College Cultural Representative for the year 2012. Currently, I am a member of 301 Productions, a group formed by ex-PICTians which sponsors one act plays of PICT for their professional shows.
