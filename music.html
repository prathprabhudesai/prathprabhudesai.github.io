Music

I am an Indian Classical Harmonium Player. I am a disciple of Shri. Rajesh Puranik and Pt. Tulsidas Borkar. I have accompanied many classical singers and tabla players over the years.

I was an active part of PICT Art Circle. I represented PICT as a musician and as a music arranger and designer in prestigious Purushottam Karandak and Firodiya Karandak. I was the Inter College Cultural Representative for the year 2012.

Currently, I am a member of 301 Productions​, a group formed by ex-PICTians which sponsors one act plays of PICT for their professional shows.


ICMDS Concert, North Carolina Art Gallery

Indian Classical Music and Dance Society, North Carolina organized this concert. I accompanied Sanjeev Ramabhadran on harmonium along with Amol Khanapurkar on tabla. This is the recording of rendition in Raga Durga. The slow composition is in Rupak Taal (7 beats cycle), medium and fast paced compositions are in Teentaal (16 beats cycle).


Diwali Pahat in Cary, North Carolina

Tabla Jugalbandi by Nihar Paranjape and Nitish Limaye, Teentaal (16 beats cycle), Raga Charukeshi.


Dance of Conflicts (Composition)

Beautifully sung by Manasi Kulkarni, this song is a mash-up of two taranas, first in Raga Patadeep and second in Raga Lalit. Ektaal (12 beats cycle).

Improvization in Raga Malkauns (Keyboards)
