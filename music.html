Prathamesh Prabhudesai

I am an Indian Classical Harmonium Player. I am a disciple of Shri. Rajesh Puranik and Pt. Tulsidas Borkar. I have accompanied many classical singers and tabla players over the years.

I was an active part of PICT Art Circle. I represented PICT as a musician and as a music arranger and designer in prestigious Purushottam Karandak and Firodiya Karandak. I was the Inter College Cultural Representative for the year 2012.

Ghashiram Kotwal

Ghashiram Kotwal is a Marathi play written by playwright Vijay Tendulkar in 1972 as a response to the rise of a local political party, Shiv Sena, in Maharashtra, India. Its theme is how men in power give rise to ideologies to serve their purposes, and later destroy them when they become useless. I was a music director and harmonium player for this play.



Date: November 25 & 26, 2016
Location: Durham Arts Center, 120 Morris St., Durham, NC 27701
