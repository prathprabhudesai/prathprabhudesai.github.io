Research

During my undergrad at PICT, I got the opportunity to work under M Selva Balan at Central Water and Power Research Station as a Research Project Intern for my final year thesis. This research lab is an integral part of Ministry of Water Resources, River Development and Ganga Rejuvenation under Government of India.

My complete research was focused on collaboration of civil engineering & hydraulics with computer vision. I worked on Mumbai Port Model where we used to perform system simulations (hydraulic models) which will be ported in real life problem scenarios.

Open Stream Water Velocity Profiler   (Project Report)

This system was developed for open-stream water velocity profiling using Image Processing algorithms influenced by motion tracking and Particle Image Velocimetry. Research was carried out on Mumbai Port Model to tackle the safety issues regarding over-flooding in the area of Navi Mumbai International Airport. Then existing hardware technologies mainly included Vertical Acoustic Doppler Current Profiler (ADCP) sensors which are costly and fail to achieve directional profiling. This non-contact technology provided a low cost and accurate solution overcoming the existing drawbacks.


Flow tracking through a colored liquid discharge

Sediment Categorization and Quantization  

Research was carried out on the sediment depositories in the coastal hydraulic model with an aim of identifying the reservoir storage capacity, advanced planning for water management and strengthening silt removal process. A new algorithm based on gamma correction was developed for the accurate detection of sediments and neural network techniques were used for the classification.


Pseudo-colored Image of Sediment Classification - Compressed(Orange), Hindered(Yellow), Flocculent (Pink), Clear Water (Blue)

Bed Profiling and Dispersion Analysis  

Analysis of topographic nature of the water channel was done using bed profiling with the help of sensor mapping and contour plotting. The dispersion coefficients were calculated for the analysis of behavior of foreign fluids in water.
